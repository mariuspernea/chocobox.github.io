About Chocoboxes Order

Design your own gift box with sweets and treats for your friends and family. Include bespoke gifts too!

Beautifully crafted boxes with a wide range of sweets.

We will design a personalised gift box with sweets and snacks that you choose. We can create custom items such as Christmas baubles or small chocolate bouquets and we can tailor these according to your preference. Chocolate is much sweeter when you share it with your loved ones!

Personal messages can be engraved on the box.

We use wood burning tools to engrave personal thoughts as part of the box. Make your gift truly special and personal with a hand-crafted message on your gift! Whether you celebrate a birthday, anniversary or a special event, we can include a message of your choice on the box to make your gift unique!

We can include your favourite gifts.

Tell us what gift to include in the box and we will do our best to accomodate your needs. Be it a bottle of your favourite drink, a perfume or beauty products, we can help create a unique, bespoke gift to impress your friends and family! The price of the additional gifts that you want included will be added to the price of the box.



Tailor your gift

We offer several options to customise your gift, such as engraving a personal message or including a gift that you choose. Your personal message will be engraved on the box so you can create a unique gift box that will last even after you enjoy its content! The gift to be included should be smaller than the box.

Size & shape

We currently offer three main box sizes, including small (15 x 15 x 10 cm), medium (20 x 25 x 10 cm) and large (20 x 30 x 10 cm). We also offer rectangular and hexagonal shapes. Prices start from £11.99 and can vary depending on your needs and whether you would like us to include a gift or a particular item.

For family & friends

Whether you would like a gift box for a child, a friend, a partner or even for yourself, we are here to help! Create a personal, unique gift for your loved ones so tell us all your needs and we will do our best to accomodate them and create a truly special gift!
